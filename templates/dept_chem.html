Vision

Advancement of knowledge in the diverse areas of chemical sciences through excellence in teaching, research and innovation with firm commitment on producing responsible human resources capable to resolve the challenges of the ever-changing and technology-based society.

Mission

  1. To impart education that would enable the students to have a clear, conceptual understanding of the basic theories and principles of the subjects of chemical sciences.
  2. Create a healthy academic environment including research oriented growth to nurture new talents, promote free flow of ideas and thoughts to pursue innovation and nurture studentsâ€™ aspirations, understanding the possibilities and limits.
  3. Allow individual students to explore their capabilities and interests in relation to their intellectual potential and ability.
  4. To develop linkages with reputed R&D organizations, institutes and industries through education and research.
  5. To create quality citizens with social responsibilities and ethical standards.
