Vision

To facilitate the future generation of planning professionals to be stalwarts of built and natural environment, providing planning services to denizens in a holistic approach.

Mission

  1. To ensure quality education imparted to the students through cutting edge planning methodology prevailing in urban world.
  2. To develop technical as well as analytical skills among the students and empowering them to meet the various challenges of society.
  3. To inculcate the ethics of urban and regional planning practices to the students.

Programme Educational Objectives (PEO)

  1. To enhance the professional capabilities of students for preparing plans and projects for the cities and regions.
  2. To undertake research on policies and processes of urbanization and sustainable urban development.
  3. To impart adequate skills and exposure to the students to enable them to handle the complex challenges of planning, design, impact assessment as well as mitigation and preventive measures.
  4. To equip students for preparing strategy for planning and governance of cities and regions.
  5. To expose the students to social, political, economic and financial dimensions of urban development and ethical dimensions of working as professionals.
  6. To develop leadership skills that will help shape the future direction of urban and regional planning

Program Outcomes (PO)

  1. An ability to correlate the multidisciplinary aspects of urban and regional planning.
  2. An ability to establish databank for the respective cities and towns.
  3. An ability to visualize the pros and cons of the urban and regional development happening around the world.
  4. An ability to possess leadership skills for making pathways in tackling hurdles of development.
  5. An ability to guide urban and regional development ensuring sustainability.
  6. An ability to understand the value of built and natural environment.
  7. An ability to protect the historical identity of the region.
  8. Understand ethical principles and social issues.
  9. Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams to accomplish a common goal.
  10. An ability to promote the aesthetics and heritage of the region.
  11. An ability to work for the upliftment of the deprived sections of our society.

About the Department

The B. Plan and M. Plan courses started in the academic year 2015-16 to facilitate the future generation of planning professionals in the State. This Department offers four years regular programme in Planning i.e Bachelor of Planning (B. Plan) and two years Master of Planning (M. Plan) having intake of 60 and 18 Students respectively. It is the only Department in the State which imparts education in the field of Planning. The Department provides quality education to the Students through cutting edge Planning methodology prevailing in urban world & develops technical as well as analytical skills among the students to meet the various challenges of society pertaining to Urban & Regional planning.
