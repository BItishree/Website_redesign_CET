Course Credit Systems

Course credit system of teaching and continuous evaluation through internal assessment with 10 point grading is followed for the undergraduates and post-graduates in common with other sister colleges of the University (BPUT), each academic year is divided into two Semesters. Duration of each Semester is of 85 Net Instruction Days (NIDs). The course curricula of B. Tech, B. Arch, M. Tech., M. Arch., MCA, M.Sc. and Integrated M.Sc. degree courses have been framed in accordance with the guidelines of the Council of Architecture for B.Arch. & M. Arch., the AICTE for other Engineering & MCA courses and University for M.Sc. & Integrated M.Sc. Besides the course curricula, there is provision for practical training, seminar and project work

Semester Regulations

Semester Regulations are provided as per BPUT norms. For further details “Regulation for Under-graduate and Post-graduate, Diploma course” may be referred to in BPUT Website. All the students are expected to know the requirements for the award of degree/diploma they have registered for including the general academic requirements and assume full responsibility for meeting them. They are expected to be constantly in touch with the concerned HODs so that the latter may watch their progress and guide them along right lines. In no case a regulation will be waived or examination made simple because a student pleads ignorance of it.

Identity Card

A Registration Card bearing the name, Registration number, Branch and photograph of the student duly signed by the registrar of BPUT, shall be issued to each enrolled student through OJEE. The college issue the Identity card in support of above. All the students are expected to carry their identity card to the college on all working days and produce the same when required. In case the identity card of a student is lost, a new one shall be issued to him/her on payment of fees as prescribed.
